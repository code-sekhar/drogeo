    Product description

    Specially developed hypoallergenic Avrilé Sensitive Care (product of Ageo.cz) with the unique Nano Formula provides high washing efficiency while being very gentle even to sensitive baby skin.

    Owing to its unique formula, it is suitable for washing all kinds of clothes and fabric, including delicate fabrics, underwear and children’s clothes, silk, synthetic fabrics and also wool, which it makes smooth.

    The laundry washing gel has a balanced composition and does not contain any allergenic ingredient; that makes it great for washing clothes of children and persons with very sensitive skin. It penetrates deep into the fabric so it can remove even the most resistant stains while being gentle to the fabric. Furthermore, it imbues your clothes with a delicate fresh scent.

    Thanks to using the Avrilé Sensitive Care laundry washing gel, your clothes will remain beautiful for a long time!

    Dosage: (volume of the measuring cup/cap is 40 ml)

    Less dirty Very dirty
    Soft water 60 ml 80 ml
    Moderately hard water 80 ml 100 ml
    Hard water 100 ml 120 ml

    WARNING: Causes skin irritation. Causes serious eye irritation. Keep out of the reach of children Wash your hands thoroughly after handling. IF ON SKIN: Wash with plenty of water. IF IN EYES: Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes. Remove contact lenses if present and easy to do. Continue rinsing. Contains: Lipase, triaglycerol, Terpinolene, Citronellal, 3,7,7-Trimethylbicyclo[4.1.0]hept-3-ene. May cause an allergic reaction. Contains: C12-14 alcohol 7EO, Sodium Alkylbenzene Sulfonate and Sodium Laureth Sulfate. Please note: Store in a dry and cool place. After washing clothes by hand, rinse your hands thoroughly and use a suitable cosmetic cream. Surfactants contained in the product are compliant with legislative requirements on biodegradability.

    Ingredients: 5-15% anionic surfactants, 5% non-ionic surfactants, 5% soap, enzymes, preservatives: 2-BROMO-2-NITROPROPANE-1,3-DIOL, perfume.
